Frequently asked questions

Why call it SELENE?

In Greek mythology, Selene is a goddess of the Moon, and EOS sister. While this defi lego can’t promise you moon alone, EOS isn’t complete without liquid staking and on-chain stake exchange.

What gives SELENE value?

SELENE is fully backed and instantly convertible to EOS. By putting EOS in the contract, same as with REX, you are entitled to your share of REX fees and voter rebates.

How secure is SELENE?

The contract is fully automated and opensource, owner keys in a 2 out 3 multisig. Once some pending upgrades are achieved, we will further distribute the keys to the most popular BPs in the contract that we can identify will help with securing and auditing the contract.

How are the SELENE developers being paid?

The contract charges 10% of what you have earned by lending to SELENE contract. The principal deposited is not touched. Developers will keep the permissions to only reduce that percentage once the contract grows and competition shows up. That way the contract can stay competitive and relevant.

Can the rules in the contract change?

We will try to keep the SELENE as predictable as possible, but we might introduce some algorithm to incentivize rebates from varying too much, in the case that the competition starts using the uniformity of promised rebates to out-compete the SELENE. Additional proxies and rotating to cover more than 30 BPs would probably be introduced at some point as well.
